''[[The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King]]'' grossed more than $1.19&nbsp;billion, making it the highest-grossing film in 2003 worldwide and in North America and (at the time) [[List of highest-grossing films|the second-highest-grossing film]] of all time. It is also only the second film to surpass the billion dollar milestone after Titanic in 1997. It is now, unadjusted, the twelfth-highest-grossing film of all time.

''[[Finding Nemo]]'' was the highest grossing animated movie of all time but it has since been overtaken by ''[[Toy Story 3]]'' in 2010, the [[Digital 3D|3D]] rerelease of ''[[The Lion King]]'' in 2011 and ''[[Frozen (2013 film)|Frozen]]'' and ''[[Despicable Me 2]]'' in 2013, and ''[[Minions (film)|Minions]]'' in 2015 leaving it currently the [[List of highest-grossing animated films|6th highest grossing animated film]] of all time and now the 6th highest-grossing animated film after its 3D release and the [[List of highest-grossing films|32nd highest grossing film]] of all time (it was once the 9th highest grossing film).

==Events==
* February 24 - ''[[The Pianist (2002 film)|The Pianist]]'', directed by [[Roman Polanski]], wins 7 [[César Award]]s: Best Film, Best Director, Best Actor, Best Sound, Best Production Design, Best Music and Best Cinematography.
* November 17: [[Arnold Schwarzenegger]] sworn in as [[Governor of California]].
* December 22: Both of the movies from the [[Matrix trilogy]] released in 2003 were shut out of [[visual effects]] [[Academy Awards|Oscar]] consideration by the [[Visual Effects Award Nominating Committee]].
* December 31 The [[Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]] mails nomination ballots in which it qualifies 254 films released in 2003 as eligible for [[Academy Awards|Oscar]] consideration.
